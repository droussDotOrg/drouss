Ablution Practice

Search non contaminated water;

Sit in a clean from all stain;

Turn to the Kaaba (the East);

- Say Bismillahi and rinse your mouth (with use of toothpicks).

- Wash hands (3 times) to the wrists, starting with the right hand.

- Rinse mouth (3 times)

- Suck water through the nostrils and reject blowing through the nose (3 times):

- Aspire with the right hand, reject with the left hand

- wash the face (3 times) inwardly formulating intend to ablution as divine obligations.

- Wash the right forearm and the left forearm (each 3 times)

- Passing the water on his head from the forehead to the nape of the neck and the forehead

- Wash your ears

- Finally wash the right foot (3 times), then the left foot in the same way

After that, look up at the sky and say:

Ash'hadu anlaa ilaaha illa-l-Laahu wahdahù Lahu wa laa shariika ash'hadu anna-s-sal-Allaahu Seyidina Muhamadane Tahanla anleyhi s-wa-wa Salama Abduhù Rassuulù .

Then say,

Allaahumma ij'halnaa mina tawaabiina waj'halnaa minal mutatahhiriina waj'halna mine ibaadika salihina wa-s-wire ad'hilna Jannati bahidna wa mina-n-NAARI Allahumma salli anla Seyidina Muhammadine anla wa wa aali Seyidina Muhamadine Sallim.

Mandatory Practices of Ablution (Farata)

They are eight in number (8) and shall be listed as follows:

1) - Formulate internally intend to fulfill this obligation when washing the face.

2) - Wash full face

3) - Wash forearm, elbow

4) - Wash the fingers separately

5) - Passing the water on all hair.

• If the hair is twisted without any artificial material, it is not necessary to undo them.

•But if any artificial material is added to the hair, it is necessary to undo the braids so that the hair can be completely wet.

Moreover, the use of artificial hair is a bad thing

This is valid only for the small ablution. If the thickness of the braids is likely to prevent the water from reaching the scalp, you must undo them in the case of purification bath even if the braids are made with natural hair.

6 °) - Wash thoroughly feet to the ankles by rinsing each toe.

7 °) - Rub each party at the time of washing using an amount of water strictly necessary.

8 °) - strictly observe the order as established; do not wait too much with the washing of the various parts, it could do the ablution cancelled (do not wait a member dry before moving to the other).

If after ablution, you remember to have forgotten a part, you must proceed to its washing if members are already dry. If you remember just (before the drying of the washed parts), you will resume operation from the omitted part to the end.

Traditional Practices of Ablution (Sunna)

They are eight in number (8) and are listed as following:

1- Start by washing hands up to the wrists

2- Clean mouth

3- Rub water into the nostrils sucking

4- Make blowing out through the nostrils

5- Then switch hands inside and outside of the ears

6- Wet your hands again before getting on the ears

7. Bring the hands to the front after having wet it to the nape

8- Do not reverse the established order of mandatory practices.

The merits of ablution

The ablution is recommended to anyone who

• Recite the Qur'an, hadith, or religious knowledge

• Invoking the names of Allah

• Visit a holy man

• Go to a market

• Falling asleep

It is meritorious to frequently repeat ablutions.

Occasionally, it is recommended to formulate the intention to pay an obligatory prayer at the repeating of an ablution. If this precaution is not taken, the ablution will not be used for any obligatory prayer or a supererogatory prayer or to do the Kaaba rounding.

If the ablution only served to read the Koran, one can pray without repeating it.

The use of the toothpick is meritorious, if possible before the praying. It is recommended to use a toothpick, it helps:

• To attract the grace of God

• Cause irritation and anger of Satan

• Remember the name of God in the hour of death

• Keep faith until the last breath

• Getting into the good graces of two (2) guardian angels

• Mitigate the throes of agony

• Increase our chances of fortune

• Sharpen intelligence

• Improve the view

• Facilitate the expression

• Facilitate digestion

• Make stronger teeth and cleaner mouth

• Embellish the hair and delaying old age
