The Purification

Among the various kinds of purification, the most important is that of the body (dianaba), when necessary.

Any adult has to purify the body whenever needed. Whoever denies this is an infidel; whoever consciously refuses without any just cause is a godless and is without credibility. If he does not repent, he will be thrown into the fires of hell.

Reasons of purification

The reasons of body purification are 4 types:

1) - Consecutive ejaculation felt with great pleasure in awaking or sleeping.

After awaking, whoever finds semen on his clothes and its flow without being conscious must do purification and must repeat all previous prayers after this finding.

Whoever discovers after awaking suspicious spots on his body or his clothes must be purified. And also, he must repeat all previous prayers after this finding.

2) - After sexual intercourse, both adults must purify themselves, even if there was no ejaculation.

- In case of a major and a minor, the purification is mandatory for man.

- If there is an adult woman, purification is not necessary if there is no secretion.

- The purification remains meritorious for the minor in both cases.

3) - A woman menstruating must be purified after its end, however small the amount of bleeding.

4) - The purification is mandatory in cases of delivering, even if the latter is not accompanied with bleeding.

How to do purification

- Obtain water free from contamination, colorless, odorless and tasteless.

- Be in a decent and clean place and turn to the East (Kaaba) and say "Bismillahi".

- Wash your right hand up to the wrist 3 times

- Then the left hand in the same way

- Wash soiled parts

- Properly wash sex and inwardly form the intention to purify according to divine recommendations

- Proceed to wash one by one the various parts involved in the ablution except for the feet which will be in the end.

- Take water in his hands and pass on her hair, neck to the front.

- Wash your head three times, making sure to penetrate water everywhere every time.

- Washing both ears inwardly and outwardly, starting with the right ear.

- Wash the neck, - wash the right side of the elbow to the knee - do the same for the left side

- Wash the right leg to the ankle - do the same for the left leg

- Wash full back

- Wash the chest and stomach

- Finally, wash both feet that should have been during ablution.

In the case of purification for Friday prayers feet should be washed at the time like ablution.

The great ablution or purification can take the place of both washing and ablution Friday, provided that the intention to be formulated before.
